 Stay Safe Online – Checklist
 Prevention Tips
>Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
>Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or official authority.
>Verify links before clicking—avoid suspicious emails, messages, or unknown attachments.
>Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
>Limit personal information shared on social media (location, phone number, college details).
>Use only secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi.
>Regularly review your privacy settings on apps and social platforms.
>Log out from shared or public devices after use.
 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
>Never approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps—receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN.
>Do not scan random QR codes sent by strangers for receiving payments—they can debit money from your account.
>Always double-check the receiver’s name and UPI ID before making any transaction.
