Social Media Responsibility Checklist 
 Do’s
Think before you post — ensure your content is appropriate and respectful.
Maintain a professional image, especially on platforms like LinkedIn.
Use privacy settings to control who can see your information.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
Be respectful in comments and interactions with others online.
 Don’ts
Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts.
Do not overshare details about your daily life or location.
